I am currently a final-year PhD student in Machine Learning and Optimization at Sorbonne Université. I work within the Department of Statistics (LPSM) under the supervision of [Antoine Godichon-Baggioni](https://godichon.perso.math.cnrs.fr/) (Sorbonne Université), [Sylvain Le Corff](https://sylvainlc.github.io/) (Sorbonne Université), and [Adeline Fermanian](https://afermanian.github.io/) (Califrais). I was also a visiting PhD student at CIRRELT, École Polytechnique de Montréal, under the supervision of [Thibaut Vidal](https://w1.cirrelt.ca/~vidalt/en/home-thibaut-vidal.html). My research focuses on the theoretical aspects of stochastic optimization and generative models. I am also interested in applications of machine learning to vehicle routing problems.


### Research Interests

- Stochastic Optimization 
- Generative Models (Diffusion Models, Variational Inference)
- Neural Combinatorial Optimization
- Vehicle Routing Problems
